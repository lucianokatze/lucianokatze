# Luciano Katze - Who Am I?

I'm a technology and IT enthusiast with a deep interest in backend web application development. I love working on the architecture that powers applications, ensuring they are efficient and scalable. Additionally, I have a strong focus on user experience (UI/UX), as I believe good interaction and design are crucial for creating impactful products.

I'm particularly drawn to projects related to logistics and accounting. For some reason, code and developments in these areas always catch my attention.

One of my hobbies is exploring the limits of what a computer can achieve. However, what I enjoy most is sharing knowledge; I can spend hours writing, reading, and learning.
